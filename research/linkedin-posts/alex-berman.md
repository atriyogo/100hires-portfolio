## Post 2: Scaling Cold Email Systems

I was talking to someone in the insurance industry recently

He read the Cold Email Manifesto and immediately signed up for the ScraperCity Lead Database

He had one major question:

"I only need 8 meetings a month to be very well off - can I start small and scale to that number, or should I go all in from the start?"

I get where he's coming from because every other marketing channel works like this:

You want to book 8 meetings from ads? Start small with a couple hundred in spend, get one meeting and then ramp up as needed

Cold calling? Make 100 calls and see where it goes

But I want to tell you...

For cold email in 2026 it's different:

It costs the same amount OR LESS to setup 100 inboxes using custom smtp to send than it would to setup 10 google domain inboxes

Doing it the old way is now MORE EXPENSIVE and less effective

So I told him - we can build the system from 2018... or we can build the cheaper and better system from 2026

And THEN we can go hard and do something that will actually get results

Almost everyone thinks like this:

Ohh I just need 8 meetings, let's aim for 6

Or let's aim for 9

NOBODY (unless their mind is trained) is thinking to aim for 80 or 100 meetings a month

But even if you utterly mess up that 80 meeting system, you will still end up with 10 meetings a month

Cold email as it stands now, is more like putting up flyers than it is like facebook ads:

* We can get your message to 5000 people for a couple hundred bucks
* We can guarantee that they see and open the message
* And all you need to do is pick the right target and write the correct message

That's it.

Cold email is a megaphone with a direct line to your target market's desk

The fixed costs are low and the effectiveness is high (once you get the puzzle solved)

And for most people - they won't need more than 5,000 sends a month to hit their goals...

Like I said most sales people and CEO's lives would be CHANGED with just 5-10 new meetings a month

But you want to nail the basics first - and that starts with having verified contacts to send to.

The ScraperCity Lead Database has 4 million+ verified B2B contacts. Pull a list in your niche and make every one of those 5,000 monthly sends count.

Are you going to let your competitors build this system before you do? Try it now.

Talk to you soon,

## Key Insight
Cold email success comes from building scalable systems and operating at high volume rather than starting small and optimizing slowly.

## Supporting Observation
Even if a high-volume system underperforms, it still produces better results than conservative outreach due to increased exposure and opportunity.
