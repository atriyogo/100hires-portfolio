## Post 1: Human SDR + AI SDR Collaboration

Two things can be true at the same time.

1. We have a killer XDR team  
2. We have a killer AI SDR program  

They operate in concert with one another, not competition.

🦸 Our human XDRs are account strategists, and spend a ton of time on the phone (our top converting channel). They have real sales skills, educating and selling people on the value of ClickUp and earning next steps.

We have ~30 human XDRs, who are the pipegen backbone and the talent bench for the company. 5 more just got promoted to AE this week!! They’ll continue the proud tradition of being among the top performing AEs in their segments.

🤖 Our AI SDR monitors 100+ signals across our PLG user base and automates outreach for world class speed-to-lead (internally, we call it “programmatic pipegen” by the way). It feeds the pipeline for our humans to call, qualify, and convert to next steps.

This motion is orchestrated by 2 people. Without the AI, data science, and automations they’ve built, it would likely take 40 SDRs to operate at this scale.

---

## Key Insight
High-performing outbound systems combine human sales expertise with AI-driven automation to scale pipeline efficiently.

## Supporting Observation
AI handles signal detection, speed, and volume, while human SDRs focus on high-value interactions like qualification and conversion.

---

## Relevance to Cold Outreach Pipeline

- Modern outbound is shifting toward:
  - AI for lead identification and initial outreach
  - Humans for closing and relationship-building
- “Programmatic pipeline generation” enables:
  - faster response times
  - higher efficiency
  - better resource allocation
- Cold outreach success now depends on **system orchestration**, not just individual effort

## Pattern Noticed Across Content
